Use the keys WAD to move the player around. Use the S key to swim or teleport downwards or slam. Press p to pause, r to suicide, and space to brake and use powerups and checkpoints.
Scroll to access the off-screen parts of large levels (shift+scroll wheel=horizontal scroll)
